About Me

Hi, my name is Abiel I am an emerging software developer at Year Up. I joined year up to pursue my dream of becoming a professional developer. Growing up I was fascinated about computers and how they function but had no computer of my own until I turned the age of 20.

I have lived in different parts of East Africa. I was born in Ethiopia, raised in Eritrea, spent my teenage years in Kenya and finished my Highschool years here in Washington. I am lucky to have experienced different cultures growing up. It gives me different perspective in life and be more understanding.

My hobbies include working out, boxing, playing soccer with friends, and hiking. Since Washington is surrounded with breathtaking forests and mountains I get to pick and choose where my next adventure will be.
